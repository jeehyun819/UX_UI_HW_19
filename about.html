Hello, I'm Jay!

Born in Seoul, spent my teenage years in Amsterdam, and settled down in Toronto ever since I got into University of Toronto.

Although my design journey hasn’t been that long yet, I strive to design for users with empathy to solve their problems. I truly believe that making extra effort to understand how people behave and feel would help bridge the gap between design decisions and the users for a better user-centered product.

I embrace all challenges to broaden my experiences, pushing myself to be a humble lifelong learner to swim in this vast UX ocean trying not to sink! (I’m a terrible swimmer in real life btw)

On my free time you can find me reading non-fictions, scrolling through fashion blogs or stock reddit, watching YouTube videos (YouTube is my cardio!), or just daydreaming.

Please hit me up and maybe we can grab some bubble tea or beer if you’re in Toronto!

Shoot me an email or find me at Instagram or LinkedIn.
